John Legend Met His Twin and We Are All Impressed by the Similarity

June 22, 2024
blog

“No, I’m not related to John Legend,” it’s the first thing singer Talakai had to say on his recent audition for the TV reality show The Voice. His resemblance to John Legend gasped all the judges.

Talakai’s The Voice audition aimed to land him a spot on the “Legend squad,” and when John was indeed the first coach to turn for the 34-year-old singer, Talakai’s wish came true. John couldn’t help but notice the striking resemblance, raising an amused eyebrow as he turned his chair. Host Carson Daly playfully quipped, “John turned for his brother!”

Talakai’s introduction covered a wide range of topics, including his challenging childhood in the foster system and how a friend’s mother adopted him, saving him from those difficult times. Despite his past, Talakai maintains a positive outlook on life and music and seems to enjoy discussing the John Legend comparisons. “If John turns a chair for me, I’m going to be, like, ‘What’s up, cousin?’” he joked.

As soon as John Legend saw Talaika, he described him as “a taller, more handsome version of myself,” despite Legend being named People’s “Sexiest Man Alive.” While he resembled John, his voice had its distinct, reedy, trebly quality. With guidance from John on becoming a more connected storyteller, Talakai could establish his legendary status.
