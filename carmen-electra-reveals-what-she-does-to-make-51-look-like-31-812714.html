Carmen Electra Reveals What She Does to Make 51 Look Like 31

June 30, 2024
blog

Carmen Electra, the iconic model, actress, and TV personality, is turning 51 and defying all expectations by looking as youthful and vibrant as ever. With her striking features and signature looks, she has been a household name for decades, and it’s no wonder why. Despite the passing of time, her stunning figure and radiant skin continue to turn heads, and fans can’t get enough of her age-defying looks.

She sticks to the same products for years.

Discovering skincare products that suit your skin can be a daunting task, but once you find the perfect match, it’s hard to resist using them consistently. The Get Over It actress revealed that she had been following the same beauty routine for years.

“I’ve had the same skincare for 20 years and have always focused on a moisturizing makeup removal, hyaluronic acid serum, and a deep moisturizing face cream packed full of high-quality essential ingredients. For me, it’s really about the consistency of a routine that has really worked for me to keep my skin healthy, surgery-free, and glowing,” she shared.

She has a strict beauty regimen.

Establishing a daily skincare routine is essential for achieving optimal results. Electra is renowned for her radiant, healthy-looking skin and attributes it to her unwavering commitment to following a consistent beauty regimen. “In my case I am completely free of [surgery] and I would say [my youthful appearance] is because of my discipline with my skincare routine,” she said.

She starts her day with lots of water.

Paying attention to your hydration levels can be a game-changer for your skin’s appearance and health. Electra credits her radiant skin to her daily water intake.

“I made it a rule that I have to drink two bottles of water before I start my day. I started drinking water and noticed a change in my body overnight. It’s easier for me now to keep the weight off, whereas before, it was more of a struggle. My skin is completely different because it’s not as dehydrated. Water has changed my life,” she admitted.

She doesn’t drink coffee.

If you’re a coffee lover, you may want to think twice about your daily caffeine intake. Although a steaming cup of coffee is the perfect pick-me-up in the morning, drinking it too often can lead to dry skin and premature wrinkles over time.

According to Electra, giving up caffeine altogether was one of the best choices she made to maintain her skin’s youthful appearance and plumpness. “I try to stay away from items that damage your skin like caffeine,” she said. “I don’t get any caffeine any more,” she added.
