Adrienne Bailon (TV Personality, Singer and Actress)

May 19, 2024
blog

Full Name: Adrienne Eliza Bailon-Houghton (née Bailon)
Profession: TV Personality, Singer and Actress

Biography: Adrienne Bailon is an American television personality, singer, and actress, known for her involvement with the girl groups 3LW and The Cheetah Girls. She has also made a name for herself as a co-host of the daytime talk show The Real, earning a Daytime Emmy Award for her role, and as a co-anchor on E! News since 2022.

Adrienne first caught the eye of Ricky Martin in 1999, leading to her joining the pop group 3LW. The trio achieved early success with their debut album reaching platinum status and securing a spot on the MTV Total Request Live tour. In 2002, however, fellow member Naturi Naughton departed following internal disputes.

Bailon's career took a turn towards acting when she landed a role in the Disney Channel original movie The Cheetah Girls (2003). While balancing her time with 3LW, now operating as a duo with Kiely Williams, she gained recognition with The Cheetah Girls franchise, contributing to the release of successful soundtracks and albums.

Despite a brief signing with Jermaine Dupri's So So Def label, 3LW eventually went their separate ways. The Cheetah Girls also concluded their run in 2008.

Bailon went on to sign a solo deal with Columbia Records. She also found work in daytime television as a co-host of The Real from 2013 until its conclusion in 2022, and entered as a contestant on The Masked Singer in 2019. She has since built on her time at The Real, joining E! News as a co-anchor in 2022.

Bailon has been open about her relationships, including a notable romance with Rob Kardashian. After ending an engagement with music executive Lenny Santiago, she married musician Israel Houghton in 2016. The couple expanded their family in 2022, welcoming their first child via surrogacy.
