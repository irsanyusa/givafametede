Full Name: Rebecca Louisa Ferguson Sundström
Profession: Actress

Biography: Rebecca Ferguson is a Swedish actress, best known for playing Elizabeth Woodville in the series The White Queen (2013). She was nominated for the Golden Globe for Best Actress in a Miniseries or Television Film for this role, but the award went to Helena Bonham Carter for her role in Burton and Taylor.

Ferguson has subsequently played supporting roles alongside Tom Cruise in the Mission: Impossible series, as MI6 agent Ilsa Faust in both Rogue Nation (2015) and Fallout (2018). She has also featured in Men in Black: International (2019) and Dune (2021).
