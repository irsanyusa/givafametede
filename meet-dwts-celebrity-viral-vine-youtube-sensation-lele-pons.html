Meet 'DWTS' Celebrity Viral Vine, YouTube Sensation, Lele Pons

The multi-talented Venezuelan singer, actress, and internet sensation, Lele Pons is set to dazzle audiences in the highly anticipated season 32 of Dancing With The Stars. With her friend and fellow celebrity Paris Hilton cheering her on, Lele is ready to take the dance floor by storm. Before we witness her dance moves on the DWTS stage, let’s delve into who Lele is and explore the details of her exciting career and journey to this moment.

Who is Lele Pons?

Lele is a versatile entertainer known for her dynamic presence on various platforms. Her rise to fame began on the now-defunct social media platform, Vine. Where she garnered a staggering 11.5 million followers, becoming one of Vine’s most popular female content creators. After Vine, she transitioned to YouTube, where her channel, boasting over 18 million subscribers as of September 2023, continues to entertain viewers with her unique brand of humor.

Beyond her online success, Lele has left her mark in the worlds of acting, music, and literature. She’s graced the silver screen, appeared in music videos, and even ventured into the music industry. Her hit song “Celoso” earned her accolades and recognition.

Her creative endeavors don’t stop there; she’s also a published author. Currently, fans can watch her personal life through the YouTube Original series The Secret Life of Lele Pons. Additionally, she also hosts the popular podcast Best Kept Secrets with Lele Pons on Spotify.

Lele is not just an internet sensation; she’s a multi-talented entertainer with a diverse portfolio. Her connections in the entertainment industry run deep, as she’s related to the famous singer Chayanne, who happens to be her uncle. Additionally, Lele tied the knot with Guaynaa in 2023, marking a significant milestone in her personal life.

Lele Pons is Already a Fan Favorite on DWTS Season 32

Lele’s journey on DWTS certainly promises to be a highlight of her career. She’s paired with the talented professional dancer Brandon Armstrong, and their goal is clear: to win the coveted Mirrorball Trophy. The news of her participation was also greeted with excitement by her fans and fellow celebrities.

Season 32 of DWTS features an eclectic mix of contestants, including reality TV stars, actors, and singers. Lele joins a star-studded lineup that includes Jamie Lynn Spears, Alyson Hannigan, and more.

Lele isn’t new to dance competitions, as she previously served as a judge on the show Dance Monsters. In this unconventional competition, dancers used computer avatars to then showcase their skills and vie for cash prizes.

Notably, Lele’s friend and fellow celebrity Paris Hilton expressed her support for the dancing journey that lies ahead. Paris also took to social media to share her excitement, promising that Lele would “rock it” on the DWTS stage.
