Priyanka Chopra was initially not dreamed to becoming an actress.

Born in Jamshedpur, India, on July 18, 1982, Priyanka Chopra initially aimed to be an aeronautical engineer, but fate had other plans. Her beauty and charisma propelled her to victory in the 2000 Femina Miss India pageant, securing her the Miss World title that same year and launching her into modeling and entertainment.

In 2003, Priyanka made her Bollywood debut in The Hero: Love Story of a Spy. Her exceptional acting skills quickly garnered attention, leading to roles in successful Bollywood films like Krrish, Don, and Bajirao Mastani, where she received acclaim and established herself as one of India’s most celebrated actresses.

Despite her Bollywood success, Priyanka harbored ambitions beyond India. Her venture into Hollywood began in 2012 with her music career, marked by the release of her single In My City, featuring will.i.am. However, it was her role as Alex Parrish in the American TV series Quantico that truly opened the doors to Hollywood. Priyanka made history as the first South Asian woman to headline an American network drama series, earning widespread acclaim and a massive global fan following.

In 2017, Priyanka made her big-screen Hollywood debut in Baywatch, alongside Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson and Zac Efron. Although the film received mixed reviews, her performance was highly praised.
