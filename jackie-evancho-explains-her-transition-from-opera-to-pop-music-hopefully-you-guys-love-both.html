Jackie Evancho has made a remarkable transition from classical opera to pop music. While her roots lie in classical music, she has successfully embraced the world of pop with her unique and powerful vocals. In a recent video response to a fan’s question about her shift in music style, she candidly explained her transition and her deep-rooted passion for singing and songwriting.

Jackie Evancho Tells The Truth About Her Changing Music Genres

Earlier this week, Evancho made a video, where she responded to a fan’s question: “Why have you gone from opera to pop?” In response, she recorded a video message to provide insight into her musical journey.

In the video message, Evancho spoke directly to her fans, addressing the question with sincerity. She explained that her transition from classical to pop music was a natural progression in her artistic journey, as she embraced the opportunity to create and perform her own compositions. For Evancho, the desire to sing the music she writes has been a lifelong dream, reflecting her passion for creative expression and connection with her audience.

“I’ve gone from classical to pop music because it’s just kind of what naturally happened,” Evancho said. “It’s the style that I write, and my lifelong goal, since I can remember, was singing the music that I wrote.”

Importantly, her shift to pop music doesn’t diminish her love for classical music. She reassured her fans by saying, “It’s not that I don’t love classical anymore. I always will, and I’ll probably always still sing it.” This statement also shows that her classical roots remain an integral part of her musical identity, and she values the timeless beauty of classical music.

Jackie Evancho’s New Single “Consequences” is Out Now

Evancho’s new single, “Consequences,” has just hit the airwaves, delighting her fans. In a recent Instagram post, she provided a sneak peek of this haunting yet solemn song about mental health, with lyrics that express the inner turmoil many people face.

Evancho’s latest release, “Consequences,” follows her previous song, “At Least I Have Santa.” This new single has stirred up a great deal of anticipation, and fans can already pre-save it on Spotify and pre-add it on Apple Music, ensuring they’ll be among the first to experience this emotional journey when it is fully available on various audio-streaming platforms.

In addition to her poignant single “Consequences,” Evancho has also embraced the holiday season with her song “At Least I Have Santa.” Her recent post on social media, in which she expresses her honor at being featured in Spotify’s New Music Holiday playlist alongside respected artists like Andrea Bocelli and David Foster, has generated excitement among her fans.
